    About Us

    Our ever synergic and skilled team consists of experienced architects, structural engineers, civil engineers, MEP consultants, Interior designers and Project managers with expertise in various domains of Design, construction and supervision.


    We provide complete consultancy services ranging from Conceptualization of design, Preparation of 2D and 3D drawings, estimations and specifications, structural drawings, supervision, project management, detailed drawings, Billing and Interior designing along with various other intricate aspects of building construction.


    We also provide construction services which includes Building construction, Material procurement and management, Labour management, Site works and surveying etc.

    Vision

    At OCTACON, our diversified team is putting in all our expertise and experience in a streamlined channel of excellence, which we strive to achieve in each and every project or work that we take up.

    Mission

    Services

    Architectural Design

    Design conceptualization, Preparation of plans, sections and elevations, Realistic 3D visualisations for an assuring concept, Working drawings, Detail drawings, Material selection and quantization, Area details, Landscape drawings, Site visits and inspections.

    Construction Management

    Material procurement, Material and labour management, Accounts for every project, Physical site supervisions at various stages, Digital site assistance 24 x 7.

    Structural Consultancy

    We provide cost-effective designs for both small and high-rise buildings without cutting corners on strength and serviceability. Our service also include proof checking of analysis and design of structures.

    Interior design

    Conceptualization of designs, Accurate 3D visualizations with material specification, 2d drawings and detailing, Interior estimation, Site execution, Site visits and Inspections.

    Estimation and Value engineering

    Abstract and detailed estimates of undertaken projects, Quantity evolution and analysis, Detailed specification, Range of options for varying cost dynamics, Rate analysis, Latest labour estimation according to the prevailing market trends, Preparation of bills of construction, material, labour, consultancy etc, Scrutiny and approval of contractor’s bills, Site verification for bill approvals, Audit on discrepancies in bills.
